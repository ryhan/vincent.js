Vincent.js

Looking for an HTML6-based node.js framework for Canadian ninja rockstar developers?
Look no further than this uneccessary js library!

<script type="text/javascript" 
	src="http://ryhan.me/lab/vincentjs/js/vincent.js"></script>
<script type="text/javascript">
	vincent().init();	// Wakes Vincent up.
	vincent().fuckYeah();	// (optional) Adjusts links on the page.
</script>

But wait, there's more! Vincent.js is also a social media platform for synergizing Vincent sightings in the cloud, and comes with an awesome API! Simply call 

vincent().sayHello();
to engage with Vincent once you've woken him up!
